About

Richmond Carpentry is a friendly, reliable carpentry services company with over 25 years experience of household woodwork, storage, flooring and kitchen fitting plus a range of commercial carpentry . This gives us planning skills for the big job and an eye for detail and quality customer service.

Based in Portishead, North Somerset we provide carpentry solutions for customers across the whole of North Somerset and beyond.

Services

We are always happy to provide free quotes for a range of jobs from small one-off home improvements to larger, ongoing commercial projects.

  • Kitchen planning and fitting
  • Bespoke furniture
  • Doors and skirting
  • Wardrobes and storage
  • Office Workstations
  • Glass balconies

We can project manage the complete kitchen installation, using the same team of trusted, plasterers, plumbers, and electricians.
